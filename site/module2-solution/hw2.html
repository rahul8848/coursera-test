What Comes with Briyani?

Briyani

Briyani is a mixed rice dish from the Indian subcontinent. It is popular throughout the subcontinent. It is made with spices, rice, lentils, meat and vegetables..

Korma

Korma is a braised spiced sauce made with yogurt, cream, nut or seed paste. There are two main types of korumas. One type contains meat. The other type contains vegetables. All of the ingredients are same for both of the types.

Raitha

Raita is an Indian, Pakistani and Bangladeshi side dish. There are two types of raithas. The first type of raitha is made with yogurt, or curd. Along with yogurt and curd raitha is made up of raw or cooked vegetables, more seldom fruit.The second type of raitha is called boondi raita. This type of raitha is made from besan or chickpea flour which is generally refered to as gram flour.
